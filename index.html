About

Engineering the Mind
of Tomorrow's Machine

Dr. Rahul Kumar Dubey is an Expert & Technical Fellow at Bosch Global Software Technologies, one of the world's largest engineering and technology organizations. His research sits at the convergence of artificial intelligence, electric mobility, and intelligent systems architecture — domains that will define the next decade of industrial transformation.

As an IIT Delhi alumnus and IEEE Senior Member, Dr. Dubey brings rare dual depth: the rigorous academic foundation of India's premier technical institution and the global deployment scale of Bosch. He has authored 24 patents — 14 as sole inventor — spanning EV charging intelligence, Vehicle-to-Grid control systems, software-defined vehicle runtimes, and cross-domain engineering platforms.

Through the ANRF GREET program, he serves as Professor of Practice at IIT Delhi — actively bridging industry innovation with academic research and mentoring India's next generation of AI and mobility engineers.
